How It Works
Fan-worn · In-venue · On-air

Co-branded LED wristbands turn every seat into part of the show — a synchronised, broadcast-ready moment that puts MyPrize blue across the entire arena.

  • Universal distribution — co-branded LED wristbands for all attendees on entry
  • 3 × in-event moments — a randomly selected wristband “lights up” three times throughout the night, locating a winning fan in-crowd
  • Winners receive co-branded PFL & MyPrize merch, presented on the night
  • TV production awareness — broadcast cut-ins to the lit-up fan moments, building three storylines through the card
  • Consistent visual branding across the arena — synced colour, logo lockup, fan-led activation
